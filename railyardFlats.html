Railyard Flats

Approach:
Field Research
Informed Brainstorming
Wireframing
SEO Strategy
Project:

Railyard Flats is a new multi-million dollar luxury and sustainable development. The developers wanted to launch a simple one-page website and logo that quickly showcased their living and work spaces for potential tenants and built momentum around the project.

Solution:

A logo mark and simple website with UX focused content that details and highlights the luxury, sustainability, and amenities of the space.

Research:

Field Research

To ensure that I understood the project, I conducted field visits of the development site. I wanted to identify distinguishing landmarks, understand the “live and work” aspect of the location, and clarify the vision behind the development. More so, I was also able to receive early renderings of the project to better understand how sustainability and luxury were interweaved.

Informed Brainstorming

After conducting the field study, reviewing the renderings, and connecting with the one of the development partners, I began brainstorming possible solutions. The development partner was passionate about ensuring users who visited the website could quickly digest the information, understand the values behind the build, and experience how luxury and sustainability were combined in the development. I began this project by creating UX content based on the needs of the developer and potential tenants. To do that, I brainstormed keywords and points for the project. Once I was able create the UX focused content, I then began creating wireframes around those keywords.

Wireframing:

One keyword idnetified was “sustainability." Based on color theory I wanted the site colors to be earthy and minimal to highlight this keyword. Another was “architectural" so I wanted something bold to emphasize the care behind the development. Based on that I added floating square elements that scrolled with the user and added an interesting visual element to the site. Finally, because we were limiting this site to one page, it was imperative to display as much imagery as possible to showcase the space.

SEO Strategy:

Lastly, we focused on SEO strategy. To do this, we incorporated branded and long-tail keywords to attract visitors with specific search intent. For example, luxury living in Sioux Falls or Luxury condos in Downtown Sioux Falls. We also ensured the site was optimized for multiple devices, images were properly sized, and our speed index was within an acceptable frame.
